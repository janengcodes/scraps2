Ingredients

For the Fruit Filling: 2 medium apples (Granny Smith or Honeycrisp recommended), peeled, cored, and diced 1 cup dried apricots, chopped 1/4 cup brown sugar 2 tablespoons cornstarch 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg 1 tablespoon lemon juice For the Crumble Topping: 1 cup all-purpose flour 1/2 cup rolled oats 1/2 cup brown sugar 1/4 cup granulated sugar 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces

Instructions

    Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch baking pan. Prepare the fruit filling: In a large bowl, combine the diced apples, chopped apricots, brown sugar, cornstarch, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Toss well to coat the fruit. Make the crumble topping: In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, rolled oats, brown sugar, granulated sugar, cinnamon, and salt. Cut in the cold butter using a pastry blender or your fingers until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Assemble the bars: Spread the fruit filling evenly in the prepared baking pan. Sprinkle the crumble topping evenly over the fruit. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the fruit is bubbly. Cool completely in the pan before cutting into bars.
